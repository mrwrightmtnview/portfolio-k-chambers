WANTED DEAD OR ALIVE

Sokko Kage
Bounty Hunter

Description: Age 25 Soryan female

Crimes: Murder, Conspiracy, Assault, Stalking

Reward: $2,000

Details: Sokko is highly skilled and has ties to the Davoran mafia. Care should be taken if she is to be engaged in combat. Do not travel to Davora if this bounty is completed.

Corano Vereta
Syndicate Godfather

Description: Age 85 Davoran male

Crimes: Murder, Conspiracy, Gun Running, Smuggling, Intimidation

Reward: $10,000

Details: Corano is the godfather of the Davoran mafia and rumor has it that he was once the dark lord Krosos. He is known to be in touch with various assassins and bounty hunters. Do not engage without the aid of law enforcement. Do not travel to Sorya if this bounty is completed.

Sereaza Sharpe
Leader of Viscan

Description: Age 27 (tecnically about 227) Durati, female vampire

Crimes: Murder, Abduction, Cannibalism, Usurpation, False Imprisonment

Reward: $35,000, honors in Duratus

Details: Sereaza resides in Viscan (location unknown) in Duratus and is rarely seen in the outside world. Being a vampire, she has high strength and speed and can quickly overwhelm opponents. Do not engage without proper armor and adequate skill in magic.

Sanguis Umbra
Vampire Lord

Description: Age unknown (at least 3000). Durati, male vampire.

Crimes: War Crimes, High Treason, Mass Murder, Murder, Terrorism, Cannibalism

Reward: $75,000, honors in Duratus and Thoria

Details: Sanguis was the instigator of the ancient Sanguisian War, known to have killed thousands of innocents. Though he is considered dormant and his location is unknown, he was once a dark lord and can be extremely difficult to predict. Rumor has it that his tomb is somewhere in Thoria. Due to war tensions, care is advised when travelling to Thoria.

Captain Ribneck
Pirate Captain

Description: Age 40 Torvic male

Crimes: Piracy, False Imprisonment, High Treason

Reward: $40,000, honors in Torvic Empire

Details: Ribneck has been known to sail around the shores of Robbers' Bay, allied with the rebelling Karrci sector. He is extremely creative and has complete loyalty from his crew. He is rarely seen on land, so a means of naval combat may be necessary.

Krastinu Vredruva
King of the Torvic Empire

Description: Age 42 Torvic male, king of Torva

Crimes: Murder, Mutilation, Assault occationing actual bodily harm, Battery

Reward: $300,000, honors in Rakonan Empire

Details: King Vredruva is a powerful and traditional Torv, meaning very deadly. Being the king of Torva, he is also difficult to reach. Anyone that accepts this bounty must be skilled and strong in their own right and must be able to take on Torvic soldiers.

Ronin Fridana
Arch-Consul of the Rakonan Empire

Description: Age 70 Rakonan male, Arch-Consul of Rakona

Crimes: Murder, Hate Crimes, Stalking, False Imprisonment

Reward: $300,000, honors in Torvic Empire

Details: Arch-Consul Fridana works his crimes in the dark, and is still relatively popular in Rakona. Though he is a powerful mage and has shown that he is a genius in combat, he is relatively secluded within his palace in Scyllis, Rakona. Being the leader, he may be extremely difficult to reach and it will require the hunter to escape Scyllis undetected.

Malum
The Dark Lord

Description: Age unknown. Current dark lord

Crimes:Conspiracy to commit murder, False Imprisonment, Incitement

Reward: $500,000

Details: It has not been discovered how to reach Malum directly, but if anyone should achieve such and prove his termination will be rewarded handsomely. Extreme caution is advised, as one would have to fight him in a realm where he has control.

Odium De Ipso
Whirlwind of Death

Description: Age 22 Norcretian male

Crimes: Murder, High Treason, Terrorism, Torture, Mutilation, Arson, Conspiracy, Intimidation

Reward: $1,000,000, honors in Torvic Empire

Details: Odium is extremely dangerous and has been known to decimate professional armies with ease and without aid and has ties to a vampire clan. Do not engage him directly without backup. Military assistance is absolutely necessary.
